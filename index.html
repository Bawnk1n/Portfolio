About Me

While I have completed the first third of the program, I am continuously learning and enhancing my skills every day. My goal is to become a proficient web developer and contribute to the development of innovative and engaging web applications.

Apart from my interest in coding, I enjoy a variety of hobbies that include mountain biking, hiking, travelling, skiing, and video games. These hobbies have taught me to be persistent, curious, and to value teamwork, qualities that I believe are essential in any profession.

I am thrilled to be on this journey of discovery and to have the opportunity to collaborate with other professionals in the field. Thank you for visiting my portfolio webpage, and I look forward to exploring new opportunities with you.
